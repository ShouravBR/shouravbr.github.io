Give PickerPY a try, it works with 7Zip & Zip archives.


Quick Guide

  1. Download: Window (Google Drive), Source (python pyw file)
  2. Extract and start PickerPY.exe.
  3. Open a Zip or 7zip archive.
  4. Use the settings tab to tune the process.
  5. Start.

Glossary

Brute force generates character specified by the user to try for the password.

Dictonary generates characters specified by a text file, like words from a list.

Prefix & suffix are the characters you remember. Like pass[unknown]word, where "pass" are the starting characters, "word" are the ending characters and [unknown] are the charactes to be filled in by the utility tool.

Save/Load can be used to pause & save the progress and load it at a later time.
